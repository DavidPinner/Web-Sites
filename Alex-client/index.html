About

Alexandra’s passion for Dentistry has driven her to focus upon specific goals with her patients’ satisfaction as the highest priority. Her main concern is her patients’ journey whilst under her care. “Informed consent is the first and most important thing before a smile makeover truly begins. A patient must be made aware of their choices and be able to make a decision based on the knowledge of how each decision will affect them. This first step leads me as clinician to a point where I can truly empower my patients as they will feel included and invested in their treatment process,” says Alexandra. “When patients understand the clinical pathway and are actively making decisions, I am then able to create tailor-made treatments. Once my work as a Dentist is done on a practical level, I still have a duty of care towards those for whom I have created a new smile. Educating a patient about maintenance and the impacts of certain lifestyles is the key to long term results."

Tailor-made Treatments

Restorative work is bespoke to the individual case.

Be it Root Canal Therapy, a Crown, Tooth Wear or Periodontal treatment, it amalgamates into a proposed plan and nearly all patients will require a multidisciplinary solution at some point in their lives.

Your Dream Smile

Clear aligner therapy or Invisalign Go as it is more commonly known, is a system that uses clear flexible trays that clip onto the tooth arch with strategically placed composite attachments.

It does not only result in a more balanced smile, but may help correct rotations in teeth that would otherwise limit the aesthetic result of planned restorative work.

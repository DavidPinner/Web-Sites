Facial Aesthetics

Facial Aesthetics

Be the Best Version of Yourself

The following treatments are non-surgical, minimally invasive and although long-lasting, they are reversible.

Wrinkle-relaxing Injections

Botulinum Type A is a natural protein complex that inhibits the function of the motor nerves, which prevents the muscles that are affected from contracting.

The injection points during anti-wrinkle treatments target the muscles that cause the skin to crease and eventually form wrinkles. The result is smoother, more relaxed skin.

Treating a patient early enough may also prevent future lines from forming.
This treatment is especially effective for frown lines, lines on the forehead and fine lines around the eyes. It can also reduce the amount of gum on show when you smile.

Dermal Fillers

Hyaluronic Acid (HA) is derived from glycosaminoglycans that are found in a multitude of our skin cells. Its main roles include scaffolding for the structures it is contained within and tissue repair.

As we age our faces change shape due to gravity, loss of elasticity and shrinkage of the fat pads such as our cheeks.

HA Fillers are placed under the skin layers to increase projection, camouflage deeper lines and bring back a youthful fullness to a more mature face.

They can also be placed in the lips, to create volume and definition.

SuneKos

SuneKos is a combination facial rejuvenation treatment for all ages and skin types.

It consists of multiple injections of non-cross-linked Hyaluronic Acid into areas of the skin which require hydration and the essential amino acids needed to maintain the extracellular matrix of healthy cells.

In combination, NutraKos creams and drinkable amino acid sachets give your skin the best chance to combat fine lines, puffiness, loss of elasticity and dehydration.

No treatment is without risk, cosmetic or otherwise, so when selecting your practitioner, make sure they have the professional qualifications that make them equipped to deal with any unwanted side effects as well as providing you with a great result. Minor corrections can make a major difference in reaching your full potential.
